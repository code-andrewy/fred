The Fredrick Legacy

Launched in 2024, the Fredrick series has evolved from a simple pet simulator into an epic space adventure.

Fredrick 1: The Clicker

Feed Fredrick to earn points. Features a shop for better snacks, auto-clickers, and persistent saving.

Fredrick 2: Emoji Chase

A fast-paced grid survival game. Collect coins and stars while dodging predators in a 60-second race.

Fredrick 3: Dodge

Test your reflexes by dodging falling spikes. Survive long enough to earn medals and temporary invincibility.

In Fredrick 4, take control of the galaxy with new combat mechanics:

Head-Bullets: Integrated cranial weaponry.
